Some important information to share with our guests

Late Arrivals

We request that anyone arriving after 11:50am please follow the designated paths to enter the seating area. The entrance will be lead into a curtained off section of the hall to facilitate an aisle entrance, and guests arriving immediately before and during the ceremony are requested to enter the seating area via the sides of the curtained pathway rather than the main aisle entrance.

Dietary Restrictions

Due to religious dietary restrictions, we will not be serving pork during the event. For any other dietary restrictions, please include the necessary information in your RSVP survey.

Music

Music played at the event will be a mix of radio friendly dance music, Jamaican music, and Afghan music. We encourage our guests to participate in dancing, and are requesting that there be no song requests for our DJ during the event.

Wedding Gifts

For our guests who would like to bring a wedding gift, we are planning to move to Regina, Saskatchewan, within the year and would like to request a gift of money where possible to ease the burden of moving. It will be difficult to pack all our things into a truck as is, and this would go a great way towards making the move easier on us. We apologize for any inconviences and thank you for understanding.

Alcohol

We are requesting that our wedding is a sober wedding. We do not plan to serve alcohol during the event for religious reasons.
